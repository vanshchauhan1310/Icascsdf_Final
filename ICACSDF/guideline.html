Author Guidlines

Instructions For Authors

  • Original papers based on theoretical or experimental works related to the above mentioned sub themes are solicited for presentation in the conference.
  • The paper should begin with title, short abstract and a list of keywords. Simultaneous submissions (papers already submitted to other conferences/journals) are not allowed.
  • All papers must follow Springer LNNS formatting guidlines.
  • Please do not submit plagiarized papers.
  • Turnitin Similarity index should be below 10%
  • Authors are invited to submit full papers in English of minimum 9 pages and not more than 12 pages (including figures, tables references) in Springer format.
  • All the papers will be submitted through on-line paper submission.
  • Paper must be in .PDF format

Copy Right Form

  • In order to publish paper in the conference proceeding, the authors are required to submit a signed copyright form for each accepted paper during registration.
