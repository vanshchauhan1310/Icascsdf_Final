Author Registration Rules & Instructions

Registration Requirement

  • At least one author per paper must register for the paper to be included in the conference proceedings.
  • If multiple authors of the same paper wish to attend, each must register separately.
  • The registered author must present the paper at the conference (physically or virtually* (allowed on the permission of the chairs only)).

    • Note:
    • Listeners do not need to submit a paper to register.
    • Listener registration is mandatory to gain access to conference sessions and materials.
    • Listeners will receive a participation certificate but not a paper presentation certificate.

Registration Process

  • Authors must fill out the online registration form available on the official conference website.
  • The corresponding author must ensure all co-authors are correctly listed in the registration form.
  • Any errors in author details may lead to rejection or exclusion of the paper from the proceedings.

Registration Fee & Payment

  • The registration fee is categorized as : Indian Delegates (Regular Author/Student Author/Industry Professional),Listner,International Delegates (Regular Author/Student Author/Other Participants).
  • Fees must be paid before the deadline mentioned on the website.
  • Payment must be made via the official payment methods (Online Transfer, Credit/Debit Card, etc.).
  • A confirmation email will be sent upon successful payment processing.

Proof of Payment Submission

  • After payment, authors must upload payment proof (transaction receipt/screenshot) through the registration portal.
  • Registrations without payment proof will be considered incomplete and will not be processed.

Document Submission for Registration Verification

  • Student Author: Must submit a valid student ID card or a bonafide certificate from their institution.
  • Regular Author: Must provide an official institutional email or employment proof or Goverment ID.
  • Industry Professionals : Must submit valid company identification or a letter from the employer.
  • Foreign Author / Participants : May need to submit a passport copy for verification.

Registration Confirmation & Conference Access

  • Once documents and payment are verified, authors will receive a registration confirmation email from conference chairs.
  • Only registered authors will receive the conference kit, participation certificate, and access to sessions.

Paper Presentation & Attendance Requirement

  • At least one registered author must present the paper during the assigned session.
  • Failure to present the paper may lead to exclusion from the proceedings and future conferences.
  • Virtual participants must ensure a stable internet connection and adhere to the online presentation guidelines.
  • No refunds will be provided after the registration.
  • If an author fails to attend the conference, no refund will be issued.
  • The organizing committee holds the right to reject or revoke registration in case of rule violations.
  • No refunds will be provided in case of disqualification.
